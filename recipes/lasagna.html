Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as rag√π, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Meat: this lasagna recipe starts with a pound of ground meat: either lean ground beef or Italian sausage.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar are added to the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg is beaten with some water or milk and is used to make the noodles adhere.

How to make lasagna step by step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
