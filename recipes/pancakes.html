Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a type of flat, round cake that is made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and other ingredients. They are typically cooked on a hot surface, such as a griddle or frying pan, and are often served with syrup or other toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour: all-purpose flour contains gluten, which is what gives the pancakes their structure. and pleases the gluten-free community.
  • Sugar: a little sugar goes a long way in helping the pancakes rise.
  • Baking powder: a leavening agent, baking powder helps the pancakes rise.
  • Salt: a pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor of the pancakes.
  • Milk and butter: Milk and melted butter lend moisture and richness to the pancakes.
  • Egg: eggs lend structure to the pancakes and act as a binding agent.
  • Vanilla: a dash of vanilla extract adds a touch of flavor.
  • Butter: melted butter lends moisture and richness to the pancakes.

How to make pancakes step by step

  1. Mix the dry ingredients: combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients: combine milk, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract in a bowl.
  3. Mix the ingredients: add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Cook the pancakes: cook the pancakes on a hot surface, such as a griddle or frying pan, until they are golden brown on both sides.
